Data Retention & Privacy

1. PUBLIC LEDGER DATA

SolScanner aggregates data from the Solana blockchain. This data is public, immutable, and permanent. We do not control this data, and we cannot delete transaction history, wallet balances, or token metadata from the blockchain, even upon request.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • On-Chain Data: Wallet addresses, transaction signatures, and token interactions.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device info (for DDoS protection and rate limiting).
  • Usage Data: Which tokens you search for and which features you utilize.

3. LOCAL STORAGE

We use local storage (cookies) solely to:

  • Store your "Watchlist" locally on your device.
  • Remember your theme preferences and UI settings.
  • Manage your API Key session if logged in.

4. THIRD-PARTY SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated traffic data with our infrastructure providers (e.g., RPC node providers, hosting services) strictly to maintain service stability.

5. GDPR / CCPA NOTICE

Because blockchain data is immutable, the "Right to Erasure" (Right to be Forgotten) cannot apply to data stored on the Solana blockchain itself. For off-chain data (e.g., your email if you subscribed to our newsletter), you may contact privacy@solscanner.io to request deletion.
